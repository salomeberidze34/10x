Batumi -The Beautiful Jewel of the Black Sea...

Batumi is located in the southwest of Georgia, along the shores of the Black Sea. As the capital of the Adjara region, Batumi is often described as one of the most beautiful cities of the Gulf of Pontus Euxine. Batumi offers a unique and harmonious combination of sea and mountains.

Enjoying a subtropical climate with lush vegetation and an abundance of palm trees, this seaside city is known as a resort destination, attracting tourists from all over the world.

The dynamic pearl of the Black Sea, known for its measured lifestyle and festive nights, enchants with an elegant combination of architectural styles of the late 19th and early 20th centuries. This gives Batumi a special Mediterranean atmosphere.
